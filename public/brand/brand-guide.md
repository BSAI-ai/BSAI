# BSAI Brand Identity

## Brand Essence
BSAI is a premium AI platform for learning, creating, working, and solving problems with clarity and confidence.

## Personality
- Premium
- Modern
- Minimal
- Intelligent
- Trustworthy
- Innovative
- Global
- Timeless
- Elegant
- Professional

## Primary Slogan
The Future, Made Intelligent

## Color System
- Primary Blue: #2563EB
- Emerald Green: #10B981
- Dark Navy: #0F172A
- White: #FFFFFF
- Light Gray: #F8FAFC

## Typography
Use Inter, SF Pro, Segoe UI, or similar modern geometric sans-serif systems.

## Logo Usage
- Preserve clear space around the symbol.
- Use the full wordmark on light backgrounds.
- Use the dark or light versions on contrasting surfaces.
- Keep the symbol monochrome for simple applications.

## Minimum Spacing
Maintain at least one symbol height of clear space around the logo.
